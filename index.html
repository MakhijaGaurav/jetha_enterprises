ABOUT US

Incepted in the year 1964, Jetha Enterprises is pioneer in the field of election materials and services. The firm was established by our beloved Mr. Mohandas Kimatrai Budhwani in 1964 and is continued by his son Mr Jethanand Mohandas Budhwani. Our organization is engaged in manufacturing and trading of wide range of and products and services which include Election Accessories, Trophies, Flags, Badges, Scalfs, Rubber Stamps and much more...
